# Brainstorm and Research Meeting on 4/19/26

Our team analyzed popular free slot machine apps, focusing on design, psychology, mechanics, and user experience. Key insights include:

- **Design & UI**: Apps mimic Las Vegas casinos with flashy, vibrant colors that excite users. However, excessive flashy lights can be eye-draining; dynamic saturation is preferred.
- **Psychology & Engagement**: Small wins with audiovisual effects trick players into feeling they’re winning while losing money. Auto-spin features minimize the feeling of losses but pause to emphasize big wins. New users often win big initially to create a dopamine rush.
- **Mechanics**: Key mechanics include RTP (return to player), volatility, bonuses, and paylines. Multipliers, free spins, and special tokens (buyable with in-game currency) can enhance engagement. Free spins may be offered during losing streaks to retain players.
- **User Feedback**: Themed variety for relaxation during downtime. Complaints focus on high minimum bets and affordability issues at higher levels. The slowing of the last reel creates an “almost win” feeling that drives continued play.
- **Improvement Ideas**: Lower minimum bets, add left-handed spin options, include periodic multipliers, reward special tokens, and offer free spins on losing streaks. Longer win animations could extend the feeling of success.
