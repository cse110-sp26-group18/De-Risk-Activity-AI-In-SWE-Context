ℹ HOW TO PLAY

🎮 Basics

  • Start with 1,000 credits. Minimum bet: 100 (multiples of 100 only).
  • Match symbols on the center payline across all 5 reels to win.
  • Top and bottom rows also pay at 50% rate (bonus paylines).
  • Need 3+ matching symbols from the left: 3=30%, 4=60%, 5=100% multiplier.

💸 Credits & Refill
