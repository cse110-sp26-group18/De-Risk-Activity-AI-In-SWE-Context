HOW TO PLAY

Basics

  • Start with 1,000 credits. Minimum bet: 100 (multiples of 100 only).
  • Match symbols on the center payline across all 5 reels to win.
  • Top and bottom rows also pay at 50% rate (bonus paylines).
  • Need 3+ matching symbols from the left: 3=30%, 4=60%, 5=100% multiplier.

Total Winnings

Tracks your cumulative session winnings. Keeps adding up across spins and only resets when a fresh session starts (after a credit refill).

Credits & Refill

  • Out of credits? Wait 30 seconds for a free refill of 500 credits.
  • 3-win streak → +2 Free Spins. 5-win streak → +1 Lucky Token.
  • Occasional consolation free spins on losses (8% chance).

Special Tokens

  • 🍀 Lucky — Biases reels toward high-value symbols; may force 3+ match.

Tokens are consumed on use. Earn via win streaks.

Star Multiplier & Periodic Bonus

2+ Stars on the payline multiply your payout by the star count. Every 7th spin randomly triggers a 2×, 3×, 5×, or 10× bonus multiplier.

Progressive Jackpot

Grows with every spin. Hit 5 Diamonds on the center payline to claim it. Resets to 50,000 after a win.
