ℹ How to Play

🎮 The Basics

  • You start with 1,000 credits. Minimum bet is 100 credits.
  • Bets must be in multiples of 100 (100, 200, 300 … up to your balance).
  • Match symbols across the center payline of all 5 reels to win.
  • Top and bottom rows also pay out at 50% rate (bonus paylines).
  • Match 3-of-a-kind from the left for a payout (30% rate), 4-of-a-kind (60%), or 5-of-a-kind (100%).

💸 Credits & Refill

  • If you run out of credits (below 100), wait 30 seconds for an automatic refill of 500 credits.
  • Win streaks of 3 award +2 Free Spins. Streaks of 5 award +1 Lucky Token.
  • Occasional consolation free spins are awarded on losses.

Tokens are consumed when used (win or lose). Earn new ones via win streaks.

⭐ Star Multiplier

If 2 or more Stars land on the center payline alongside a win, your payout is multiplied by the number of Stars. Every 7th spin also randomly triggers a bonus multiplier (2×, 3×, 5×, or 10×).

📈 Progressive Jackpot

The jackpot grows with every spin (faster on wins). Hit 5 Diamonds on the center payline to win the entire progressive jackpot. It resets to 50,000 after being won.
