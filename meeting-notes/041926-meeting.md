## Slot Machine Research Brainstorm
We held a brainstorm session with our respective research for slot machine app 
ideas. 

Aila: There was a minimum bidding amount, the spin button is on the right which 
is biased towards right handed players and doesn't account for left handed people 
or those with limited use of right hand. People are having fun and de-stressing 
while we work on it

Ayat: Noticed auto-spin feature which allows players to not feel losses as 
severely since it automatically goes to the next round, but emphasizes wins and 
rewards. 

Tim: Researched color schemes and psychology behind it (e.g. red is more volatile, 
purple is more luxurious.) Dynamic saturation vs flashy lights, but perhaps caution 
the amount of flashy lights as it can be draining on the eyes. Features that should 
include flashy lights are buying tokens and spin, to draw more attention.

Solaiman: looked into RTP and volatility. RTP is return to player percentage, or 
the amount of money programmed to return to players over time. Higher RTPs are 
generally valued. Frequent wins are more popular with those who need money.
Aome screen is more vibrant, more lights when actually playing

Howard: Special modes and animation.

Aaron: Noticed that the last slot slows down in terms of speed--plays into the 
player mentality of almost winning and drives the urge to continue playing / betting. 
Creates a dopamine rush when it seems like you will win. 

Anvay: Include multipliers (maybe based on periodice intervals), special tokens 
(increase probability of wins or jackpot, can be bought with regular tokens), and 
free spins to offer if player is on a losing streak

# Personas and Users

## Personas
### Persona 1: Bruno Mars, 40 y/o
**Background:** Singer and entertainer, former gambler, and Las Vegas lover

A Las Vegas lover and former gambler who thrives on the thrill of the casino floor. He brings his performer's eye for spectacle everywhere — nothing underwhelming will do.

Wants & Needs:
- Wants to have the same fun as he would gambling in Vegas without the actual risk
- Enjoys flashy visuals and rewards
- Wants the feeling of winning

Does Not Want:
- Does not want to gamble with real money
- Confusing or underwhelming interfaces
- Games that differ too much from an actual casino game

---

### Persona 2: Omair Qazi, 22 y/o
**Background:** Graduate college student and Teacher's Assistant for a Software Engineering course

A software engineering TA juggling coursework and grading. After long hours staring at code, he wants a quick mental reset — something exciting but easy to pick up and put down.

Wants & Needs:
- A no-brainer game he can play after long hours of grading assignments
- An exciting game that is thrilling but also not stressful

Does Not Want:
- Features that are not explained
- Games that differ too much from an actual casino game


## 5 User Stories
1. Invite friends + start a competition party
2. As a rational person, I know I can’t win money from the game, but I will play for several rounds everyday just for fun and to see my luck, sometimes to kill time. So I need tokens supplied everyday, and the UI looks comfortable, rather than like the fancy ones in Casino.
3. I play often after work as a way to have fun and have a good time, but constantly seeing the same UI gets boring. I want to be able to win new themes and new winnings to customize my experience to something I like. Also having the ability to spin the slot machine anywhere makes it easier for me after working with my hands a lot and gives similar vibes to casino play. 
4. As a competitive player, I want a ranking system to be able to play against my friends so I can compare my performance.  I also want a starter package that I get for joining because of a friend's referral so I feel rewarded joining the game. I want ways to collaborate with my friends, like rewards for sharing big wins with friends.
5. As a bored student in lecture, I want a simple, fast, high-excitement, game that I can conveniently pick up and play, So I can ease my boredom without having to commit to a long playthrough or face a learning curve
